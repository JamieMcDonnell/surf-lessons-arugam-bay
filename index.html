Qualified Surf Teacher in Arugam Bay.

I started to surf at 12 years old, and this amazing sport became to be my life passion

I love to share this passion with you. So I studied and passed the ISA surf exam, which is world organization for surf teachers to be prffecient and provide serious surf lessons.

I now have 15 years of experiences as a surf teacher. Because I love surfing I would like you to love it too and to see you making progress and have fun.

I am happy when you are happy and when you catch your own wave after my coaching is my target. I like to do proper coaching, so if there is bigger group, I take my team of teachers to take care of you, so each student will have enough attention and training.

I speak English fluently, so there will not be any missunderstanding during lesson :)

Qualified to ISA/ILS Standard.
